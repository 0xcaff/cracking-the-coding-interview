Lockers with an even number of factors below 100 are closed. Lockers with an
odd number of factors below 100 are open.

8 is closed (1, 2, 4, 8)
9 is open   (1, 3, 9)
16 is open  (1, 2, 4, 8, 16)
25 is open  (1, 5, 25)

If the square root of a number is an integer, it is open.

1^2, 2^2, 3^2, 4^2, ..., 10^2

10 lockers are open
